Codeway Runners

Android studioJava

A prototype mobile game freaturing an endless runner mobile game that incorporates roguelike elements into its swipe-based gameplay. Players navigate three lanes by swiping left, right, up, and down to avoid procedurally generated barriers. The core loop involves collecting randomly spawned coins and power-ups, such as magnets, while progressing through the run. A defining feature is the roguelike progression system, where upon reaching certain score milestones, players choose between two randomized upgrades, such as increased movement speed or extended magnet duration. The game also includes quick-time events that players must complete to continue their run, and features achievement tracking and a post-game leaderboard. (I created the power-up system and its power-ups, collectibles, roguelike upgrades, quick timed events, player accounts based on usernames, moving background)

2D AI simulation

OpenGLC++

A small project where i tried coding out a turn-based simulation game with fog of war mechanics, entities with their own unique finite state machines. The core of the simulation is based on an action point system that governs every entities' movement and combat per turn, this is supported by an economy where miner entities have to mine materials to spawn different entities. The units are driven using A* star and Depth first search pathfinding and they have to navigate a randomly generated tile-based map where in-game events can happen to change tiles to other tiles costing different amounts of ap for entities to move to.
